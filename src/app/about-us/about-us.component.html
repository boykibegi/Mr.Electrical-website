--------------------------HEALTH & SAFETY --------------------

MR.Electrical Co Ltd understands the importance of safety in the workplace, and on-the-job. Providing a drug-free, healthy, safe and hazard free work environment, as well as an on-going company sponsored Safety Program is our number one Priority. We employ the services of an expert Safety Inspector /Trainer / Coordinator regularly and provide O.S.H.A compliant, training for both new and veteran employees. The Safety Inspector also coordinates routine safety and equipment inspections at all of our job sites, and holds weekly safety meetings.

Each of our work sites is equipped with Emergency First Aid supplies as well as Personal Protective Equipment (PPE), and every member of our field personnel team is trained in First Aid and Adult CPR

We constantly evaluate and implement new programs and training strategies in an effort to reach our ultimate goal of totally eliminating occupational injuries. We believe that this goal is realistic and achiev- able. Since our incorporationwe have maintained a phenomenal record having no on-job accidents.
